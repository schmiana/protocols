Reference
[RFC3820]
Note
This is the official IANA registry of Proxy Certificate Policy
Languages that may be contained in X.509 Proxy Certificates as
defined in [RFC3820].

Authors of additional policy languages are encouraged to publicly
document their policy language and list it in this registry.

Each policy language is identified by an OID. The reference must
define both the syntax and the semantics of the policy language.
