Global Positioning System (GPS) a system of satellites that use spread-spectrum transmission, operated by the US military for commercial and military applications gps L1, L2, L1C, L2C, L5 ["Navstar GPS Space Segment/Navigation User Interface," ICD GPS-200, Apr 2000.]
