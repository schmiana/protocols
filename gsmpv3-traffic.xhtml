0b00 Indicates that the Traffic Control is not available in the Capability Set. [RFC3292]
0b01 Indicates that the Traffic Control is applied to all connections that use the Capability Set. [RFC3292]
0b10 Indicates that the Traffic Control is available for application to connections that use the Capability Set on a per connection basis. [RFC3292]
